Slime Slayer

Date: May 2024

Role: Solo Developer

Tech Stack: Unity, C#, WebGL

Created as a 2-day university project, Slime Slayer challenged me to turn a basic 3D scene into a small but engaging game, blending my early Blender rig animations with Unity’s FSM for smoother movement and enemy AI. Absolutely Realistic Physics Bowling

Date: April 2024

Role: Solo Developer

Tech Stack: Unity, C#, WebGL

A 2-day university project, this physics-based bowling game was a fun exercise in realism, built to simulate the exact feel of a real bowling lane with custom physics materials, pin reactions, and a directional power meter. I applied SOLID principles to make the code base manageable, and was surprised at how quickly I could produce a playable game. Feedback on the final game helped me see how true-to-life simulation doesn’t always equal playability, shifting my approach toward balancing realism with player enjoyment.
